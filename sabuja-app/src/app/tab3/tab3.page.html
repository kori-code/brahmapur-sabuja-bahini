Who We Are

Brahmapur Sabuja Bahini (BSB) is a dedicated environmental NGO working towards making Berhampur greener, cleaner, and more sustainable. Since our inception, we've been actively involved in tree plantation campaigns, waste management drives, and community awareness programs.
Mission

To promote environmental awareness and sustainable living through community action and innovative conservation strategies.

 Vision

Building a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable Berhampur for future generations through collective community effort.
